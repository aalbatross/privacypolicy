WealthYogi Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 11, 2025

At WealthYogi, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the WealthYogi mobile application (“App”).

1. No Data Collection

WealthYogi does not collect, store, or share any personal or financial information. All data you enter — such as assets, debts, FX rates, and net worth — is stored locally on your device only.

2. No Account or Cloud Sync

The app does not require you to sign up or log in, and it does not connect to external servers or cloud storage. Your data never leaves your device.

3. Local Data Storage

All information remains on your device. If you uninstall the app, your data is permanently deleted.

4. Analytics and Tracking

WealthYogi does not use analytics, cookies, or tracking tools.

5. Third-Party Services

The app does not connect to or share data with any third-party services.

6. Permissions

WealthYogi may request local storage access to save your financial information securely on your device. No other permissions (contacts, location, camera, etc.) are required.

7. Security

Your security depends on your device’s protection (e.g., passcode, Face ID, encryption). We recommend enabling built-in device security.

8. Children’s Privacy

WealthYogi does not target children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal data.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we update how data is handled (for example, adding optional cloud sync), we’ll update this policy and notify users before changes take effect.
